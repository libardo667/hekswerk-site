The roadmap

We'll walk through 4 phases together to form a coherent plan.

Each phase narrows uncertainty into decisions, handoffs, and concrete next actions.

01

Orientation & assessment

We figure out where you actually are. Timeline, household, budget, how much bandwidth you've got, and what pathway makes sense

02

Legal & administrative orientation

The legal and paperwork layer. What to file, what to ask your attorney, what order to do it in.

03

Logistics architecture

The physical move itself. Getting there, getting your pets there, finding a place to land, and having a plan B.

04

Landing & integration

Your first 90 days in your new home. Getting integrated, both socially and practically.

Boundaries

I prefer to stay in my lane.

Map dependencies, organize timelines, prepare questions for providers, identify handoff points, and help you move through the plan without holding everything in your head.

What I don't do

Legal advice, tax advice, financial planning, therapy, realtor work, veterinary work, travel-agent work, or immigration form preparation.
