Primary focus

Relocation planning for lives that don't fit generic advice.

Especially useful for self-employed people, disabled or chronically ill folks, neurodivergent planners, LGBTQ+ households, pet owners, couples, and families coordinating a move with real constraints.

The work is simple to name.

You bring the tangle. I help turn it into a roadmap: what's important, what depends on what, and what you can safely ignore for now. We can work together on the whole thing, or just the parts that feel most overwhelming.
