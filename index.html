What Hekswerk does

Soft systems belong in the plan.

Legal, tax, money, documents, housing, shipping, healthcare, and arrival logistics all matter. So do capacity, identity, household dynamics, support continuity, and the cost of trying to hold too much alone.

A planning practice, not a filing service.

Hekswerk does not file your visa application, register your business, or do your taxes. The work is to map the whole move so attorneys, accountants, movers, and housing providers can do their narrower jobs at the right time.
