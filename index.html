Process

Clear steps, visible progress, and a handoff you can live with. Calm operator energy.

1. Map

We map the workflow

Inputs, owners, edge cases, and failure points. We name what “done” means before we touch tooling.

2. Build

We build a reliable prototype

We prioritize traceability and maintainability. If it can't be debugged, it doesn't ship.

3. Harden

We test and harden

Error handling, fallback paths, and permissions. Your system should fail gracefully, not theatrically.

4. Hand off

We deliver docs and a walkthrough

Hand-off docs, screenshots, and a short walkthrough. You keep the keys, not us.

Principles

Guardrails that keep the work from turning into glittery chaos.

Privacy-first

Consent-based access, least privilege, and clear credential boundaries. You own your data and accounts.

Audit trail

Readable logs, durable trackers, and “why did this happen” answers. Traceability over vibes.

Maintainable

Simple patterns, clear naming, and documentation. The system should survive your busiest week.
