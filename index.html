The first steps

A calmer way into the conversation.

The intake is not a sales funnel. It is a way for me to understand the shape of your move before asking for your time.

01

Tell me what is going on.

A short form gathers your location, target country, timeline, household shape, and what feels hardest right now.

02

I read it myself.

No list sale, no automated drip campaign, no handoff to a closer. I review your situation directly.

03

We decide the next right step.

If there is a fit, I reach out personally to learn more and discuss whether a roadmap would help.

Who is behind this

I am building this from inside the move.

I am Levi Banks, founder of Hekswerk. I am in the active execution phase of my own DAFT-based relocation from Portland, Oregon to The Hague, with six years of planning behind me and arrival planned for November 2026.
