What Hekswerk does

Soft systems belong in the plan.

Legal, tax, money, documents, housing, shipping, healthcare, and arrival logistics all matter. So do capacity, identity, household dynamics, support continuity, and the cost of trying to hold too much alone.

I plan the move; the specialists do the filing.

I won't file your visa, register your business, or do your taxes; that's what your attorney, accountant, mover, and housing providers are for. My job is to map the whole move so each of them does their narrower part at the right moment, and nothing falls through the gaps between them.

Methodology

About Levi

Built from inside the move.

I'm Levi Banks, a relocation systems planning advisor in Portland; I'm also in the middle of my own move to the Netherlands (late 2026), so most of this I'm living in real time. I work with Americans navigating complex moves to the Netherlands and other parts of the EU.

"Hekswerk" is Dutch for a witch's work, the kind of thing that looks like magic from the outside. That's the wink in the name; the actual work is the least mysterious thing I can make it. I take a move that feels like an impossible tangle and turn it into something you can see, hold, and act on. The magic, if there's any, is just careful systems work done out loud.

I'm autistic and ADHD, and I live with chronic illness. This practice is built for people who share some of that profile; the ones for whom a move can't run on brute force and a full tank of energy. I built Hekswerk because the relocation advisor I would have wanted for myself didn't exist yet, so I'm becoming them.
