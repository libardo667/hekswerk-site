Hekswerk Relocation Coaching

Structured planning for Americans relocating to Europe — built for households that don't fit standard expat advice.

You're seriously considering relocating from the US to Europe. You've realized this process touches dozens of interconnected systems — immigration law, housing, pet logistics, financial planning, healthcare, municipal bureaucracy, language, identity, family — and trying to research all of it simultaneously is overwhelming. You don't need another blog post. You need someone who has mapped the system and can walk you through it without letting you drown.

Who this is for

Built for households whose specific lives don't fit standard relocation advice well: self-employed people, neurodivergent planners, chronically ill folks, LGBTQ+ people, pet owners who refuse cargo travel, couples and families coordinating multiple nervous systems through a multi-year transition.

What you receive

A 4–6 week engagement producing a comprehensive personalized planning system: Relocation Systems Map, Critical Path Timeline, Dependency Map, Researched Provider Shortlist, Document Readiness Checklist, Budget and Risk Register, First 90 Days Landing Plan. Three live calls; bounded async support throughout.

Our Process

Order emerging from mystery — applied wherever the system is too tangled to hold in your head at once.

1. Map

Define the scope, surface the dependencies, identify the leverage points. One coherent picture, before any tooling or bookings.

2. Build

Implement the plan. Privacy-first by default. Durable structures, not brittle glue.

3. Handoff

Documentation, walkthroughs, decision support. You understand it because it was built to be understood.

Systems Map / Workflow Map

Visual diagrams of moving parts, triggers, and dependencies.

Critical Path Timeline / Logic Build

The actual sequence of decisions, bookings, or automation logic.

Dependency Map / Test Plan

Which decisions block which other decisions; how we know it works.

Provider Shortlist / Runbook

Vetted external help, or instructions for operating the system.

Documentation & Walkthrough

Plain-language docs and Loom recordings — for both engagement types.

Credential & Decision Handoff

You own the keys, the accounts, and the final calls.

About Hekswerk

Hekswerk is a one-person practice run by Levi Banks. After six years of preparing my own DAFT-based relocation from Portland, Oregon to The Hague — currently in active execution, with arrival planned for November 2026 — I'm formalizing the methodology I've developed into an advisory practice for other Americans navigating complex international relocations.

The automation work that Hekswerk has historically offered continues: privacy-first, single-workflow builds for small businesses and independent professionals. Both services share a foundation in systems thinking — the practice of seeing how the parts of a complex situation actually connect, and building structures that respect human understanding.

Hekswerk is run as an eenmanszaak (Dutch sole proprietorship) under the DAFT pathway.
