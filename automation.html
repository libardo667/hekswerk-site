Hekswerk Quickstart

Privacy-first automation. One workflow at a time.

Hekswerk builds clear, maintainable automations for small businesses and independent professionals who want fewer loose ends — without handing over control of their systems.

Each Quickstart engagement focuses on one well-scoped operational workflow, built carefully and handed off with documentation you can actually use.

No full operational overhauls. No ongoing monitoring retainers. No mystery automation you can't understand. Just steady craft.

What's included (every tier)

  • Workflow MappingA clear, human-readable map of the process being automated, including triggers, outcomes, and edge cases.
  • Automation BuildImplementation using the most appropriate tool for the job — often Zapier, Make, or self-hosted n8n — with a preference for privacy-respecting, client-owned systems.
  • DocumentationPlain-language written documentation explaining what the automation does, how it works, and how to modify, pause, or disable it.
  • Loom Handoff VideosRecorded walkthroughs of how the workflow operates, how to troubleshoot common issues, and how to safely extend it in the future.
  • Client OwnershipAll accounts, credentials, and access remain with you. No hidden dependencies or vendor lock-in.

Pricing tiers

Basic — $750

For simple, low-risk automations.

Typical: 1 trigger · 1–2 connected tools · minimal branching · low data complexity.

  • Form → email + spreadsheet logging
  • Simple intake routing
  • Basic reminders or notifications

Standard — $1,500

The most common engagement.

Typical: multi-step workflow · 2–4 connected tools · conditional logic or branching · light error handling or logging.

  • Email intake → parsing → record creation → notification
  • Status-based follow-ups
  • Multi-tool coordination with clear handoff points

Complex — $2,500+

For workflows with higher complexity or sensitivity.

Typical: multiple branches and edge cases · messy or inconsistent data · API usage or advanced logic · compliance- or risk-sensitive contexts.

  • Legal-adjacent intake workflows
  • Document handling with validation
  • Multi-actor or approval-based processes

Final pricing is confirmed after scope review.

What's explicitly out of scope

To keep projects durable and predictable, Quickstart does not include:

  • Full business process redesigns
  • Multi-department or enterprise systems
  • Ongoing monitoring, analytics, or maintenance retainers
  • "Always-on" automation supervision

Additional workflows can be scoped as separate engagements.

Who this is for

Quickstart is a good fit if you want:

  • Clarity over cleverness
  • Systems you can understand and own
  • Automation that reduces cognitive load without creating fragility

Especially well-suited to small service businesses and legal-adjacent operations.

The Hekswerk philosophy

Automation should respect human understanding. No opaque systems. No brittle glue. No surprise behavior. Just careful work — designed, built, and handed over with intention.
